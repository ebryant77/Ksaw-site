Information Technology

To guarantee that our students obtain the best education possible, our curriculum includes courses in information technology, computers, and software engineering.

Through the selection, design, application, integration, and management of computing technologies, we aim to address the needs of IT users in an organizational and societal context. The BSIT program at Kennesaw State University is recognized by ABET, which means it has met the high-quality requirements set by the Information Technology profession.
Our BSIT, BASIT, and MSIT degree programs can all be completed online. This is a fantastic option for students who don't have time to attend traditional classes on campus due to their hectic schedules, such as those in the military or who live outside of the Metro Atlanta area.
